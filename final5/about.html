Being the most densely populated place in New York City, Manhattan has a population of 1.629 million people. Which is the equivelent to almost 70,000 people per square mile. Manhattan is also very big on bussiness & shopping... including Wallstreet and Times Sqaure.

Broadway, Wall Street, Empire State Building, Greenwich Village, Harlem, Central Park, World Trade Center, MOMA, Columbia and New York University and the Metrolpolitan Opera House.
